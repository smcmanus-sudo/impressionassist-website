Built by Radiologists, for Radiologists

ImpressionAssist was created by a practicing radiologist who understands the daily challenge of patient communication. After seeing countless patients confused by their radiology reports, and fielding endless phone calls from referring physicians asking for simpler explanations, we knew there had to be a better way.

Our mission is simple: bridge the communication gap between complex medical imaging findings and the patients who need to understand them. We believe every patient deserves to understand their own health information.
