Why I Built This

I've been reading radiology studies for over 25 years. I had no idea that patients understood only 50% of what I wrote in my reports, or that about 42% of my follow-up recommendations never happen. And, honestly, if I did, I'm not sure what I could've done about it. Until now.

ImpressionAssist uses artificial intelligence to convert my confusing impressions into patient-friendly language.

I've added an "Action Items" line to make it easier for referrers and patients to know what I want them to do moving forward.

A friend of mine had an MRI that mentioned a "pleomorphic adenoma versus Warthin's tumor" in his parotid gland. He's a smart guy. He called me panicked because he couldn't tell if he was fine or had cancer. My goal is to make sure this doesn't happen again.

Radiologist & Founder
